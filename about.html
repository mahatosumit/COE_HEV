Who We Are

At the Centre of Excellence in Hybrid & Electric Vehicles, KPRIET, our mission is to bridge academia and industry with project-based learning and international certifications. Our curriculum spans EV powertrains, battery management, safety, diagnostics, and charging infrastructure. We operate with a strong focus on hands-on training, reverse engineering, and real-vehicle experimentation.

Global IMI-aligned assessments & badges
Industry workshops (AUDI, Mercedes-Benz demos)
Battery & BMS, motors, inverters, CAN diagnostics
Green mobility projects & entrepreneurship

Our HEV Lab features cutaway engines, EV kits, diagnostics benches and charging setups to provide total system understanding, from cell to system level.

Hands-on Curriculum

Our modules cover EV fundamentals to advanced topics: powertrain architectures, battery chemistry & BMS, charging standards (AC/DC), inverter & motor control, thermal management and vehicle integration. Learners work on real assemblies, simulators and diagnostics to bridge the theory–practice gap.

Key Highlights & News

Milestones and activities from our Centre

India’s First IMI Approved Centre Inaugurated

Certification
Our COE became the first IMI-approved centre in India, aligning training standards with IMI (UK). The centre now offers IMI Level 1 and Level 2 award pathways with global digital badges.

AUDI Q5 Engine Cutaway — Learning Exhibit

Workshop
Students explore engine architecture, drivetrains, safety protocols and diagnostics through a hands-on exhibit and guided lab walkthroughs.

Skill Training Programs (Painting, CAD, Reverse Engineering)

SkillUp
Short modules aimed at practical skills for mobility careers — CAD modelling, vehicle painting workflows, teardown & assembly, and service best‑practices.

International Collaboration with IMI (UK)

Global
Learners gain assessment pathways mapped to IMI standards and access to a global network of 500+ centres.
