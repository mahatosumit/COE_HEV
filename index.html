Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to reveal the answer.

What is IMI and why does it matter?
IMI (Institute of the Motor Industry, UK) provides globally recognized automotive qualifications. Our IMI-approved centre ensures your learning and assessment meet international standards.
Do I need prior EV experience?
No. Start with IMI Level 1 for fundamentals and safety. Level 2 tracks build diagnostics and service competence.
Are there industry workshops?
Yes. We host sessions with partners like Audi and run exhibits such as engine cutaways, charging demos and safety drills.
How do I register?
Use the Book a Visit button or any Register button on this page. A popup will open with the application form.
Do you provide placement support?
We connect qualified learners to internships and job opportunities through our partner network and alumni.
Can institutions arrange custom visits?
Yes, we arrange tailored lab tours and mini-workshops for faculty, students and industry teams.
Is safety gear provided?
We provide standard PPE for training sessions; you will also learn proper safety protocols and handling.
